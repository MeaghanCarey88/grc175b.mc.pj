Frequently Asked Questions
Q.
How do you use the juice concentrates?
A.
To use our juice concentrates, simple use a 1:1 of our juice concentrates and water, then mix them together.
Q.
How do you store the products?
A.
Our juice concentrates can be stored on the shelf or in the refridgerator.
Q.
What are the products made with?
A.
Our juice concentrates are 100% fruit blended concentrates with no additives or artificial sugars.
Q.
Do I need a contract form to order?
A.
To order our products, no contract is required. To place an order with us, a minimum of case must be ordered.
Q.
Are your products FDA compliant?
A.
Yes, our products are FDA compliant.
